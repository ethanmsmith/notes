# Banana Mug Bread

## Ingredients
 - 1 Banana
 - 4 tbsp flour (oat, ww, almond, etc.)
 - 1/4 tbsp baking powder
 - pinch of salt
 - 1 tbsp nut butter
 - 1 tbsp honey

## Directions
1. Combine dry ingredients. Mash banana and other ingredients.
2. Combine all and mix until no more dry ingredients.
3. Add to slightly greased mug.
4. Cook in microwave 1-2mins
